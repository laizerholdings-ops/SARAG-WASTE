About The
Company.

SARAG WASTE BAYBACK CENTER NPC is a community-based recycling and buy-back centre located in Douglas, Northern Cape. Established with a vision for environmental sustainability, we serve as the primary link between local waste generation and large-scale industrial production.

Our focus extends beyond mere collection. We are dedicated to creating economic opportunities for informal waste collectors, households, and businesses. By purchasing recyclables at competitive prices, we directly support local income generation and community empowerment.

Frequent Questions

1. What materials do you buy?

We purchase PET/HDPE plastics (drink bottles), cardboard boxes, paper, and aluminium cans. All materials should be empty and relatively clean.

2. How is the price determined?

Pricing is based on current national market rates for recyclables. We weigh your materials on-site on precision scales to ensure you get the exact fair value.

3. Is there a minimum weight requirement?

There is no minimum for drop-offs. Whether you have one bag or a truckload, we are ready to process your recyclables.

4. Do you collect from businesses or schools?

Yes. For bulk industrial or educational recycling drives, we can arrange specialized logistics and collection schedules across Douglas and nearby areas.

5. Where is your center located?

Our main hub is located at 1165 Dirang Street, Bongani, Douglas, Northern Cape. We are open and active for all community members.
